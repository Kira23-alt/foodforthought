Best Ever Guacamole




For all my fellow avocado toast advocates, looking for a way to level up a basic staple, the humble avocado, look no further!

This recipe is the best guacamole you will ever make, period.

Prep time: 10 mins.

You will need:


    Ingredients
  • Avocado (1 or 2)
  • Red onion (half)
  • Jalapeños
  • Tomato (1)
  • Coriander
  • Garlic stems
  • Black pepper
  • Rock salt
  • Sweetcorn
  • Kidney beans (optional))
    Method
  1. Start by dicing the red onion, jalapeños and the tomato.
  2. Next, slice the avocado down the middle (vertically), you should be able to separate the two halves of the avocado and remove the pip from the avocado.
  3. Next take each half of the avocado and with a sharp knife cut lines into the fruit both vertically and horizontally.
  4. Then spoon out the avocado of its shell into a bowl.
  5. Next take a fork and slightly mash the avocado.
  6. Add your red onion, jalapeños and tomatoes.
  7. Next strain your sweetcorn and kidney beans and add to the mix (the tinned kind works well!)
  8. Finally season with black pepper and rock salt and give the guacamole a good stir.
  9. Throw on some fresh coriander for a flavour packed punch.
  10. Enjoy on top of toast (classic!), or salad, or as a dip with cheesy nachos (yum!) or sweet potato fries (trust me), or whatever you want, the sky’s the limit with this best ever guacamole!
