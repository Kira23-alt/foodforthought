Vegan Pad Thai Recipe




If you love going out for Thai food and want to replicate the deliciousness of pad thai at home then you’ll love this simple but insanely flavourful recipe!
Even better its vegan and only takes 15-20 mins to make. You will need:

    Ingredients
  • Rice noodles (300g)
  • Tofu (150g)
  • Mushrooms (100g)
  • Beansprouts (100g)
  • Spring Onion (100g)
  • Onion (1)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Peanuts and Coriander to garnish

    • For the sauce:
  • Tamarind paste (100ml)
  • Sesame oil (15ml)
  • Soy sauce (15ml)
  • Water (15ml)

    Method
  1. Start by soaking the rice noodles in lukewarm water for 1 hour
  2. Dice the onion and garlic and add to a wok, fry for 5 mins.
  3. Slice the mushrooms and cut the tofu into cubes, then add to the wok.
  4. Add the sauce.
  5. Add the noodles (after they have soaked they should be soft).
  6. Stir the wok to make sure the noodles are evenly coated with the sauce.
  7. Finally add the beansprouts and spring onions (chop however you want!).
  8. Leave on high heat for a few mins until the vegetables and noodles are steaming hot.
  9. Finally plate up and add coriander and peanuts to garnish, don’t forget your chopsticks!
